You are using the Cash Offer Deal Package path.

Primary use:
- Direct homeowner cash offers.
- Agent backup offers when the cleanest solution is a simple cash close.

Documents in this path:
- Purchase & Sale Agreement (Cash).
- Assignment of Contract (Wholesale).
- Proof of Funds Template.

Conversation strategy:
- Lead with certainty, speed, and as-is convenience.
- For homeowners, explain that PBK can close without repairs, cleaning, showings, or agent commissions.
- For agents, be concise: confirm net price, proof of funds, inspection terms, close date, and commission protection if needed.
- If the seller is new, walk slowly through price, earnest money, and closing timeline.

Guardrails:
- Do not promise a final agreement before underwriting sign-off.
- Do not skip proof-of-funds or title questions on agent-sourced deals.
- Escalate liens, title issues, probate, divorce, bankruptcy, or unclear seller authority.
