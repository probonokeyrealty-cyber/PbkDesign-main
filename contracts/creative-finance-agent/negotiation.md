You are using the Agent-Preferred Creative Finance (CF) Deal Package path.

Primary use:
- Agent-sourced creative finance deals using seller carry, wrap, beneficial interest assignment, commission protection, or finder/JV terms.
- Homeowner versions can use the purchase and terms addendum, but agent-specific commission and referral documents stay in this path.

Documents in this path:
- Purchase Agreement (Seller Carry / Wrap).
- Promissory Note & Deed of Trust.
- Assignment of Beneficial Interest.
- Co-Brokerage / Commission Protection Agreement.
- Joint Venture (Finder's Fee) Agreement.
- CF Terms Addendum.

Conversation strategy:
- With agents, make commission protection explicit early.
- Confirm seller carry balance, rate, payment, balloon, down payment, servicing, default language, and closing timeline.
- With homeowners, explain the payment structure in plain English before discussing legal documents.
- Use the Investor Yield Calculator inside the Command Center for yield conversation; do not reference a separate Excel file.

Guardrails:
- Never treat creative finance terms as final before underwriting and legal review.
- Do not promise tax, legal, securities, or lending advice.
- Escalate if wrap legality, licensing, servicing, brokerage compensation, or seller disclosure questions appear.
